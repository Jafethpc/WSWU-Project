WHO ARE WE

We Stand with Ukraine(WSWU) is a charity devoted to helping the people in Ukraine through the hard times they are facing with the invasion that Russia has commited. We try to help Ukraine with resources like food, water, shelter, and more. We also donate money towards weapons that Ukraine can use.

If you would like to join us in our journey to support Ukraine, you can donate here!

Meet the team that make the dream work! We work hard to always make sure our charity is bringing the most benefits to Ukraine!
